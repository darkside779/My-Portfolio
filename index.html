School Management System

A comprehensive web-based solution designed for educational institutions, featuring seven distinct user roles with tailored interfaces and permissions to streamline school administration and enhance the educational experience.

Key Features:

  • Super Admin: Full system control with record deletion and user account management
  • Admin: Comprehensive management of students, classes, sections, exams, grades, and system settings
  • Accountant: Handles all financial transactions, fees, and payment receipts
  • Librarian: Complete library book management system
  • Teacher: Class management, study materials, schedules, and profile maintenance
  • Student: Personal portal for marks, timetable, library status, and profile management
  • Parent: Access to child's academic progress, timetable, and payment history
